This suggests the community has reached a critical threshold: we have enough capable LLMs and agent frameworks, and the bottleneck is now **cost, latency, and context management**. headroom's proposition—reducing 60-95% of tokens without losing answer quality—directly addresses the economic reality of LLM usage at scale.
